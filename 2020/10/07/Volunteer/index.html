Volunteer

volunteering

The most meaningful campaign in this National Day Holiday must be the volunteer activity.
This is our work

This is also my first time to be a volunteer, like most student, I also feel a little nervous.
Am I qualified for the job?
Will people be satisfied with my service?With an uneasy mood, I walked to the door of the Ming Xiaoling Museum.
I, together with two a-level student. Our job are selling the guides and giving out the questionnaires.

We can change our job for several times during the three hours period.

The museum is so small that I can go through all the galleries in five minutes. I handed out the questionnaire, running in each galleries again and again, upstairs and downstairs, just to find more people. The visitors were also very understanding, and they were generally willing to take a few minutes to fill out a questionnaire. Although the problems are not very interesting and attractive at all.

We finally succeeded in distributing 130 questionnaires, which was also appreciated by teachers.

I felt so happy and proud too.
