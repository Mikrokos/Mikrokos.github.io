About coffee

咖啡总结

我们的咖啡一共有三种，分别是纯拿铁，抹茶拿铁，草莓拿铁，比较意外的是抹茶拿铁卖的最好，其实一开始打算多做草莓拿铁的，草莓也准备了很多，可以做装饰。至于价格，真的是非常便宜，纯手工咖啡，高品质牛奶和配料这个给了我十足的信心。

出现情况是因为一开始销售不佳，半天才卖出去几杯，然后我就开始思考，首先是调低定价，然后再加上用喇叭喊，在校园里四处游荡，最终卖的还是不错。

最令人高兴的还是大家的高评价，很多老师也品尝了咖啡，并且给出了赞扬。我很享受美食节这个活动，也希望以后可以推出更多高品质美食。

There are three kinds of coffee, namely plain latte, green tea latte and strawberry latte. Surprisingly, green latte is the best seller. In fact, at the beginning, we planned to make more strawberry latte, but we also prepared a lot of strawberries, which can be used as decoration. As far as the price is concerned, it is really very cheap. Handmade coffee, high quality milk and toppings give me full confidence.

What happened was that the sales were not good at the beginning, and only a few cups were sold in half a day. Then I began to think, first of all, I lowered the price, and then I used the loudspeaker to shout and walk around the campus, and finally the sales were still good.

The most gratifying thing was the high comments from everyone. Many teachers also tasted the coffee and gave their praise. I really enjoyed the festival and I hope there will be more high-quality food in the future.

Introduction of me

A short introduction

Speaking of me, the first thing that comes to mind when describing myself is that I am really ordinary. This means I have many common hobbies like others and do not always enjoy the success in my life. But failure sometimes can teach me more. Therefore, this is the reason why persons like me always keep trying which seems they never give up.

However, almost everyone have different opinions on me. Some teachers say that I am a quiet and shy, but those who know me well will not agree. In fact, I am outgoing and helpful.

I like to do a variety of activities. For instance, I like playing the piano and also listening to music. In addition, I spend some time listening to music. Moreover, I have a good sense of humorous so people never feel bored with me. I am full about the unknown since it is the main way that people learn the world.

Next, My proudest moment was passing my level 10 piano exam when I was in fourth grade at primary school; it took me three months to prepare for and I worked very hard. In general, it only takes me three months to get such a brilliant grade. Because my parents promised to give me a puppy as a gift if I achieved excellence, it was like working hard for my dream. When I passed the exam, I was extremely proud of this achievement and it demonstrated that I was determined and able to achieve goals and win the thing I prefer most.

This is me, a special and normal girl.

The causes of Rwandan genocide

The Rwandan genocide, also known as the genocide against the Tutsi, was a mass slaughter of Tutsi, Twa, and moderate Hutu in Rwanda, which took place between 7 April and 15 July 1994 during the Rwandan Civil War. (From Wikipedia)
We can first have a look at some background about the genocide. Back to the period after World war ll, revolution and Hutu–Tutsi relations after independence makes the two ethnic group had been in a state of confrontation for a long time, and then Rwandan Civil War happened in the 1980s, also at 1990, a pogrom was organised on 11 October in a commune in Gisenyi Province, killing 383 Tutsi.(From Wikipedia) It seems that the conflict between the Hutu and Tutsi become more and more serious. I considered this is the first cause.
After that, let us turn to the major cause, most of the experts think it is the immediate cause. On 6 April 1994, the airplane carrying Rwandan President Juvénal Habyarimana and Cyprien Ntaryamira, the Hutu president of Burundi, was shot down as it prepared to land in Kigali, killing everyone on board.(From Wikipedia) The genocidal killings finally began the next day. Soldiers, police, and militia quickly executed key Tutsi and moderate Hutu military, they also recruited and pressured Hutu civilians to arm themselves with machetes, clubs, blunt objects, and other weapons and encouraged them to rape, maim, and kill their Tutsi neighbors and to destroy or steal their property. (From Wikipedia) This was the main event of the Rwandan genocide. And I think it is the second cause, also the most recongnized. Someone think the attack was a premeditated murder.
All above are the internal reasons, that means it happened because of the country itself, I also find some other causes.

For example, why they had conflict? Maybe there is something different between the two ethnic groups. There were people whose religion, culture, habits are all different. However they had to live in the same area which could not make both two agree with the other. Another important reason was due to the United Nations. As we all know one of the duties is prevent fighting and violence from happening, but it did not finish the job well. The United Nations Assistance Mission for Rwanda (UNAMIR) had been in Rwanda since October 1993, with a mandate to oversee the implementation of the Arusha Accords. UNAMIR’s Chapter VI mandate rendered it powerless to intervene militarily, and most of its Rwandan staff were killed in the early days of the genocide, severely limiting its ability to operate. UNAMIR was therefore largely reduced to a bystander role, and Dallaire later labelled it a “failure”.(From Wikipedia)

And now we need to talk about the genocide in two concepts. One is the realism, which emphasizes competition and conflict in international relations. The state is the major role and also the justice and theoretical ethics.. Otherwise, liberalism emphasizes cooperation and morality in international relations. In this perspective, a country is a collection of different domestic social interest groups.

**The cause of the ethnic conflict can be contributed to the competition between the two different national in the same state. It involves national security interests and power disputes at the same time. The event turned violent because there was no peaceful solution.

Volunteer

volunteering

The most meaningful campaign in this National Day Holiday must be the volunteer activity.
This is our work

This is also my first time to be a volunteer, like most student, I also feel a little nervous.
Am I qualified for the job?
Will people be satisfied with my service?With an uneasy mood, I walked to the door of the Ming Xiaoling Museum.
I, together with two a-level student. Our job are selling the guides and giving out the questionnaires.

We can change our job for several times during the three hours period.

The museum is so small that I can go through all the galleries in five minutes. I handed out the questionnaire, running in each galleries again and again, upstairs and downstairs, just to find more people. The visitors were also very understanding, and they were generally willing to take a few minutes to fill out a questionnaire. Although the problems are not very interesting and attractive at all.

We finally succeeded in distributing 130 questionnaires, which was also appreciated by teachers.

I felt so happy and proud too.

The sycamore tree

The sycamore tree has been at the top of the hill forever. It was on a big vacant lot, giving shade in the summer and a place for birds to nest in the spring. It had a built-in slide for us, too. Its truck bent up and around in almost a complete spiral, and it was so much fun to ride down. My mom told me she thought the tree must have been damaged as a sapling but survived, and now, maybe a hundred years later, it was still there, the biggest tree she’d ever seen. “A testimony to endurance” is what she called it.

A discussion

I had an interesting argument with my parents, the phare sycamore has more than one meaning. It represents two different trees. One is wuhuaguo tree in Chinese, and the other is wutong tree. The bilingual translated it into the first meaning，but my parents do not agree，common sense told them people cannot climb on the wuhuaguo tree since they are too tiny.
