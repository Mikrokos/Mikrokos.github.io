4.The spore fermentation medium was introduced to prepare a large number of spores

5.Centrifugal precipitation was left standing

6.Heat by water bath of boiling water

7.Get the purified spores

8.Gradient dilution

9.Apply to the surface of AGAR and place in an incubator

10.Count and compare the result
