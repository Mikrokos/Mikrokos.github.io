Garbage classification

Since November 1, 2020, Nanjing has begun to enforce garbage classification, and residential areas have started to put garbage into designated places. It was expected that citizens would be dissatisfied with this regulation, but in fact, it did not make people complain like Shanghai before, and people accepted this regulation and actively carried out garbage classification.

So how should we practice garbage classification in schools?
First of all, it is natural to start from the individual. We should have a sense of classification in our hearts. For example, we should prepare garbage bags by ourselves, place them in a unified way, and clean the garbage at the garbage dropping point at a fixed time every day.

Second is to strengthen education, campus often waste generated in the limited range, is just some used waste paper, snack bags, cans, yogurt, milk cartons, if you do not know what these respectively belong to waste, can search, or use the class meeting to get to know, so that it can achieve the campus of garbage classification.

Moreover, the unique classification method of each class. Buying small sorting garbage cans is the most common method. In this way, in the process of throwing garbage, people’s classification consciousness can also be strengthened and thinking can be triggered.

Finally is to everyone’s initiative, separate garbage is not a person, is a collective, garbage sorting is easy, but you always worry about this matter, not a I literally throw that’s all right, anyway, nobody knows what is I throw, classification is a sanitation worker or the helper, if so, class’s and grade’s garbage classification must do bad, regular appraisals garbage classification can improve class honor outstanding class and the desire to do it well.
