Plans

Dear all

Hello!

I am a student from PAL2. And now I am glad to write you an email about my plans during the three years in Jinling High school.

I will divide my plans into three main parts.

1. PAL

1. Schoolwork and IELTS

Try to adapt the new environment and the study condition. I did not do well in the September monthly test, and I think the most important things I need to do is make progress in the coming Mid-term test. I plan to attend the firs IELTS exam next summer since I think I need to prepare a lot. For next year I will attend the IGCSE test.

\2. Club

I think I should put my effort in some meaningful clubs, so I only attend three clubs. I will take part in the FBLA exam and I am also a member of Model United Nations. Another club is just for fun, I choose it through my interest. Moreover I plan to be a volunteer in the Mingxiaoling Museum and I have already passed the first test.

\3. Extra part: I plan to do a further study in social psychology and attend the reading seminar.

Also I want to join the Student Union.

2. AS

\1. Schoolwork

Attend AS test.

Also I plan to attend a biology test in AQA system and get the score.

\2. Club

Start a club of my own.

\3. Extra part: Complete the draft of recommendation letter. Choose the university and major. (Initial thoughts: UK UCL&LSE Biomedical engineering& biomedical science& Psychology)

3. A2

\1. Schoolwork

Prepare the application materials, plan the exams and ask the foreign teachers to estimate the grades.

\2. Apply for a university

Decide which school you want to go to, then pay the deposit and apply for a visa.
