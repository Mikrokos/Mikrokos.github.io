Introduction of me

A short introduction

Speaking of me, the first thing that comes to mind when describing myself is that I am really ordinary. This means I have many common hobbies like others and do not always enjoy the success in my life. But failure sometimes can teach me more. Therefore, this is the reason why persons like me always keep trying which seems they never give up.

However, almost everyone have different opinions on me. Some teachers say that I am a quiet and shy, but those who know me well will not agree. In fact, I am outgoing and helpful.

I like to do a variety of activities. For instance, I like playing the piano and also listening to music. In addition, I spend some time listening to music. Moreover, I have a good sense of humorous so people never feel bored with me. I am full about the unknown since it is the main way that people learn the world.

Next, My proudest moment was passing my level 10 piano exam when I was in fourth grade at primary school; it took me three months to prepare for and I worked very hard. In general, it only takes me three months to get such a brilliant grade. Because my parents promised to give me a puppy as a gift if I achieved excellence, it was like working hard for my dream. When I passed the exam, I was extremely proud of this achievement and it demonstrated that I was determined and able to achieve goals and win the thing I prefer most.

This is me, a special and normal girl.
